Introduction

Tyler's Donut Shop

Tyler's Donut Shop is a family run bakery that specializes in donuts. It is located in San Luis Obispo and has been open since 2000. Cookies and pasteries are also sold at Tyler's Donut Shop.

Target audience

The people that use this site are customers that are looking to order some of the menu options available. These customers are new and returning customers.

Their primary task and goals are to discover what items are on the menu and where we are located so that they can call to place an order to pick up.

About Us

Perfecting the best donut recipe since 1999, Tyler Goodman opened up his own donut shop in 2000. Tyler's Donut Shop is a family run bakery that specializes in donuts, however your favorite cookies and pasteries can also be found here. All the donuts, cookies, and pasteries are freshly made every morning with perfection. So don't be shy and stop by your local donut shop today!
